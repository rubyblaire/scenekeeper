# SceneKeeper

***SceneKeeper*** is a lightweight, SFW roleplay assistant for FFXIV designed to help players keep active scenes organized, readable, and easy to follow.

Built for roleplayers, venue staff, companions, hosts, storytellers, and anyone who wants a cleaner way to track who they are writing with during an active scene.

---

## ✦ Features

### ***Scene Partner Tracking***

Keep a simple roster of the characters currently involved in your RP scene.

- Add and remove scene partners
- Track character names during active scenes
- See which tracked partners are currently nearby
- Keep your scene focused without losing track of participants

---

### ***Scene Log***

SceneKeeper can help collect relevant RP chat into a cleaner local scene log.

- Capture RP-focused chat lines
- Select and review scene messages
- Filter scene activity more easily
- Copy selected scene context when needed
- Clear the log between scenes

---

### ***Scene Notes***

Use the built-in notes area to keep track of important scene details.

Examples:

- Current scene premise
- Character intentions
- OOC reminders
- Scene tone
- Hooks to follow up on later
- Important names, places, or details

---

### ***Nearby Partner Detection***

SceneKeeper can show whether your tracked scene partners are currently nearby, helping you keep an eye on who is physically present in the scene space.

This is especially useful for:

- Busy venues
- Group RP
- Companion sessions
- Event scenes
- Multi-room roleplay

---

### ***Soft Visual Markers***

Optional soft markers can help identify tracked scene partners on screen.

These are intended to be subtle, clean, and non-intrusive.

---
